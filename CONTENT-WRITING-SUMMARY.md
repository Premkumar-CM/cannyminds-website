## ‚ö° Quick Template (8 Sections Per Page)

### SECTION 1: Hero
- **H1 heading** (6-10 words)
- **Sub-heading** (20-35 words) - pain point + solution + benefit

### SECTION 2: Industry Overview
- **H2 heading**
- **Paragraph 1** (100-150 words) - Current industry state, trends, why digital transformation
- **Paragraph 2** (100-150 words) - How CannyMinds helps

### SECTION 3: Key Challenges
- **H2 heading**
- **4-6 challenge cards**, each with:
  - Title (3-5 words)
  - Description (30-50 words)

### SECTION 4: Our Solutions
- **H2 heading**
- **4 product descriptions** (one for each):
  - CannyECM for [Industry] (50-80 words)
  - CannyHR for [Industry] (50-80 words)
  - CannyScan for [Industry] (50-80 words)
  - CannyTrack for [Industry] (50-80 words)

### SECTION 5: Benefits
- **H2 heading**
- **6 benefit cards**, each with:
  - Title (2-4 words)
  - Description (30-50 words)

### SECTION 6: Use Cases
- **H2 heading**
- **3-4 use cases**, each with:
  - Title (5-8 words)
  - Company type
  - Challenge (40-60 words)
  - Solution (60-80 words)
  - Results (3 bullet points with metrics)

### SECTION 7: Why Choose CannyMinds
- **H2 heading**
- **4-6 bullet points** about our credibility

### SECTION 8: Final CTA
- **H2 heading**
- **Sub-heading** (20-30 words)
